# AmbientOS Vision

## Problem

Today's smart devices work independently.

Users need different apps for lights, cameras, AC, and sensors.

There is no unified AI brain.

---

## Solution

AmbientOS connects all devices to one intelligent system.

The AI understands:

- Who
- Where
- When
- What

It can automate actions without user intervention.

---

## Example

Person enters room

↓

Motion detected

↓

AI checks time

↓

Night

↓

Turns ON lights automatically
